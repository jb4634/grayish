About

Grayish is a lifestyle brand that designs, markets, and distributes boardsports-oriented products. The company primarily produces clothing, footwear, accessories, and related products for young men and women.

Skateboarding

Skateboarding is one of the most popular sports and has inspired its own culture. Our team riders are very experienced in park, vert, street and even contest skateboarding.

Surfing

Surfers catch ocean, river, or man-made waves, and glide across the surface of the water until the wave breaks and loses its energy. We are providing equipment best suited for enjoying the oceans.

Snowboarding

Snowboarding, winter sport with roots in skiing, surfing, and skateboarding where the primary activity is riding down any snow-covered surface while standing on a snowboard. Our snowboarding equipment and clothing is extremely cosy and good looking.

Windsurfing

Windsurfing is a surface water sport that combines elements of surfing and sailing. We provide very light and beautiful equipment and clothing.
